The Purpose of Simply Veritas

We all fall into patterns of habitual web surfing and our own cognitive biases to get information and form opinions. Social media companies not only exploit that, but also influence you to then become the carrier of these ideas. Instead of sharing commonality with borders or humanity, the role of misinformation and algorithmic design to media recommender systems have now polarized and forced us to support and resonate ideologies for the benefit of stakeholders that influence the truth. Simply Veritas aims to change this by making peer-reviewed and automated bias with veracity metrics to help you better understand the common themes in your sources. This ports into a platform that engages discussions on topics with quantitative results with the rest of the community to build collective knowledge.
